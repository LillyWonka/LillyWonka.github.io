What is BeFit?

BeFit is a source of useful information that will help you become and stay healthy and fit

The importance of the healthy lifestyle cannot be underestimated.

Healthy habits, such as eating well and doing sports regularly is a root of your well-being, weight controls, clear skin, and even good mood.

At the same time, the choice of the diet, intensivity and regularity of physical activity is individual. It's based on many factors, such as your own aims and preferences, to your health conditions and BMI index.

Ideally, right diet and physical activity plan should be created by professional nutriologists and sport trainers. But not everybody can invest time and money on this, and so amterials from the Internet come on stage.

Within such a variety of different artciels, opinions, and approaches, it's quite hard to choose one that will fit you. That's why we're here!

Everyday, our team surfs the Internet to find insightful articles on sports and nutrition. We watch hundreds videos about fitness on YouTube to find best for you.

On the basis of your personal goals and BMI, we pick up the most releavant and useful information. We thoughtfully curate and fact-check all information, so you can be sure we choose only true and the most interesting information from the Internet.

Our service is absolutely free. Log in, choose your preferences, and enjoy new materials every Wednesday and Friday
